Relevance?

Have you ever considered the life of Jesus? I think it’s profoundly relevant to you, and for a number of reasons.

No matter what has been done or is part of your past, present and future, Jesus unconditionally accepts all who come to him with love. This includes you.
Jesus cares about your heart. He does not judge based on "works", achievements or success. In our busy, success driven culture, this is incredibly relevant.
Understanding who Jesus is will help you understand yourself, as you grapple with Jesus’ interactions with other people, and reflect yourself.
Jesus claims to be God, not "up in the clouds" but here on earth, someone who you can study and get to know.
Some people desire a safe place to practise spirituality & mindfulness. Jesus recognises spiritual needs, incorperated prayer regularly, and had a connection to nature.
Jesus provides a good framework for making life decisions.
Jesus leads the way in physical resurrection; an end to death and suffering.
Sacrifice, at the cross, is at the heart of Jesus' life. Taking responsibility, giving something up for the sake of good is something you should also be motivated to do.
